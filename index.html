The Wordoir rules:

to win this daily puzzle, try to guess all the hidden 5 words. Here is how:

  • Choose a row to type in by clicking on it, and guess the 5 letter word with no repeating letters.
  • The right letters will show up with a grean background.
  • To help you, if the letters of the word you guessed
    do also belong to the other 4 words, the letters will show up on the other rows.
  • To make it more challenging, some of the letters that were shown to help you, may disapear unless you guessed them yourself.
  • Green means right, but Even if some of the letters are shown, you have to type a complete 5 letter word with no repeating letters at each guess, look at 'Guess displayed here' above the grid.

    • A good tip would be to try your guesses on different rows!

    GOOD LUCK
